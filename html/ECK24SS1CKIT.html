Built-In Drip Tray

The sleek, stainless steel flush-mount drip tray is built into the worktop to handle any spills or overflow. Any excess liquid drains into an internal catch bottle for easy clean up.

Forced Air-Cooled Beer Tower

The ECK24SS1C uses an internal fan pipe to redirect cold air up into the tower, preventing foam while ensuring for a completely chilled pour from keg to glass.

Large Capacity Interior

Due to its large interior, this commercial refrigerator can hold up to four sixth barrel commercial kegs. It can also fit full size half barrel kegs, oversized beveled edge kegs, pony kegs or two slim quarter kegs.

Optional CO2 Knockout

The optional CO2 knockout (with plug) allows you to connect CO2 or nitrogen tanks externally to save interior space or allow for larger tank options.

Forced Air Refrigeration

The commercial grade forced air refrigeration system distributes cold air throughout the unit to maintain the desired temperature and eliminating possible hot spots to ensure the perfect pour with every dispense.

Secure

The included built-in key lock helps provide an extra layer of security to prevent unwanted use or changes in regulator settings.

ETL-S Listed

The Edgestar ECK24 commericial kegerator is ETL-S listed to NSF-18 standard for commercial use. The NSF-18 standard establishes sanitation requirements for the materials, design, construction and performance of equipment that dispense beverages.

Two Year Warranty

Unlike other commercial companies, EdgeStar validates this unit's warranty for both commercial and residential use.
